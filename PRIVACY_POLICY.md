# Privacy Policy for Thunder YDS

**Last Updated:** February 11, 2026

## Introduction

Thunder YDS ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles information.

## Information We Collect

**Thunder YDS does NOT collect any personal information.**

All data is stored locally on your device and includes:
- Your daily learning streak
- Words you've studied
- Quiz progress

This data never leaves your device.

## AI Question Generation

Our app uses OpenRouter API to generate quiz questions:
- Questions are generated anonymously
- No personal data is sent to the AI service
- Only vocabulary words are transmitted for question generation

## Third-Party Services

We use the following services:
- **OpenRouter API**: For AI-generated quiz content
- **Netlify**: For hosting our question generation service

These services do not receive any personally identifiable information.

## Data Storage

All app data is stored:
- **Location:** On your device only
- **Method:** Local device storage
- **Retention:** Until you delete the app
- **Backup:** iCloud backup (if you have it enabled)

## Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
